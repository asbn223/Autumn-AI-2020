Rules and Regulations

1. Be respectful. Treat everyone with respect. Hate speech of any kind will not be tolerated, regardless of religion, sexuality, gender etc. No sexual comments/harassment, pornography, gore/death/extreme violence, content related to self-harm or harming others, threats/ bullying or animal cruelty. Trolling is not encouraged.

2. As a part of the Discord community, always remember to conduct yourself in a responsible manner and treat others the way you'd like to be treated.

3. Don't share Personal information. This is to protect your privacy as well as the privacy of other community members. We are not responsible for any issues you face on sharing personal information.

4. Don't purposefully try to incite drama. Avoid bringing up or "joking" about sensitive and divisive topics including (but not limited to): Politics, Religion, Controversies, Sexuality, Suicide/Death, Ethnicity/Race, Mental/Physical Impairment. Do not antagonize or encourage users to violate the rules, or purposefully try to get a specific user punished.

5. No advertising of any kind. No unsolicited DMs or ads to other members. No advertising/linking other Discord servers. No links to questionable sites.

6. Do not spam the chats. Spam does include excessive emoji use, excessive caps spam/all-caps, excessive Discord formatting, messages spanning multiple lines that are designed to interrupt or derail conversation, posting the same message across multiple channels, and inciting spam. Put the right content into the right channel.

7. Keep names and profile appropriate. Profiles with inappropriate avatars and usernames are not allowed.

8. Don't be annoying. No useless role or user pings. Do not mention moderators or the admin unless they are actively talking in the chat. Don't ask same questions again and again.

9. Keep memes in the meme channel. We like having things organized.

10. The moderation team reserves the right to remove messages and users from the server that are detrimental to the discussion and community. Please do not argue with moderators on their actions. If you disagree with a moderator action, please DM ModMail. You can be punished for something not listed here. Ignorance of the rules does not excuse breaking them.
